Smokey Kiebasa

Why kielbasa?

Kielbasa is a delicious and smokey sausage that makes a hearty and earthy meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5# kielbasa
  • 2tb butter
  • 3tb sugar
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 sweet potato, chopped
  • 3c sauerkraut, drained
  • fresh chopped parsley
  • honey mustard

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the grill to 400 degrees F.
  2. Cut kielbasa and grill for 4-5 minutes until evenly charred the ntransfer to a baking sheet.
  3. Add butter, sugar, sweet potato and onions to carmelize. Add sauerkraut until wilted.
  4. Toss together and serve with honey mustard.
  5. Enjoy!
