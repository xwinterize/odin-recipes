Onigirazu Recipe

What is Onigirazu?

Onigirazu is a delicious Japanese sandwich. It's easy to make at home and take to work.

Ingredients:

For the Wraps:

  • 2 servings sushi rice
  • 6 pieces nori seadweed

For the Sandwich:

  • 1.7 ounces red cabbage
  • 2 tb rice vinegar
  • .5 teaspoon salt
  • 3.5 ounces oyster mushrooms
  • 3 pieces sweet sausage

Preparation:

Follow these simple steps for delicious onigirazu!

  1. Slice red cabbage into fine strips and mix oyster mushrooms, with rice vinegar and salt.
  2. Add sesame oil to a hot pan and fry the sausage evenly.
  3. Place an even amoutn of sushi rice over the nori, then layer each ingredient as evenly as possible.
  4. Add a final layer of rice over top then gently fold corners or nori up and over to seal the sandwich.
  5. Wrap in plastic wrap for nice square shape, cut in half and garnish with desired toppings (sesame seeds, kewpie mayo, sweet chili sauce, etc.)
  6. Enjoy!
